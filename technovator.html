Introduction

Sustainable products are those products that provide environmental, social and economic benefits while protecting public health and environment over their whole life cycle, from the extraction of raw materials until the final disposal. Various corporate companies nowadays claim their products to be sustainable, some on the grounds of life cycle while some on resource base of the product. But in one way or the other, from manufacturing to supplying the product, any process in use deviates from the path of sustainability.

Do you think that you’ve got an idea of a product that fulfils the attributes of sustainability? A product that completely aligns with the concept of sustainability and which can stand worthy on every aspect of sustainability? Then you’ve found the correct platform to present your idea before the world. Come, submit your concept to us and if found viable and realistic, you will be mentored by experts who will help you to transform it into reality.

Techno-vator is the international product concept challenge which invites innovative ideas to realise sustainable products in our daily life. The event will be conducted online through the website of the fest and the participant needs to register on the website first and then submit his abstract of the product concept.

Problem Statement

Think and design a product that fulfils the attributes of sustainability and promotes the ideology of a greener planet. The product must be environmentally, socially and economically sustainable. You need not to submit the actual or final design of the product but the idea or a basic setup of it that explains its components and working.

Gameplay

The participants will have to submit a product concept of a device that tends to align with the concept of sustainability. The submission will be online on the mail address of the fest.

We will be declaring some topics on which the idea of the device should be based for eg. green energy, recycling of products etc.

This event is to be conducted in a single round. The participants need to submit an abstract which should provide the following information:
1. Introduction of the idea/concept
2. Basic design of the product
3. Components of the products
4. Material of manufacturing of the product
5. Working of product
6. How will it improve sustainability?
7. Cost analysis
8. Future developments of the product
These all points should be specifically included in the abstract. The submitted abstracts will be presented before a panel of experts. The panel will decide the winning concept on the grounds of realistic approach, effectiveness, efficiency, cost, robustness etc.
