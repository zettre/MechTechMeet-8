About

What if your alarm clock wakes up you at 6 a.m. and then notifies your coffee maker to start brewing coffee for you? What if your office equipment knew when it was running low on supplies and automatically re-ordered more? What if you could switch on your room heater or air conditioner even before you enter the house? Ever thought if it is possible? The answer is yes……

"Anything that can be connected will be connected." This is going to be the key line of future technologies and the name given to it is ‘Internet of Things’".

Keeping in mind the need of the time, we introduce in this season a workshop on ‘Internet of Things’ in association with and to be delivered by experts from IIT Kanpur. Internet of Things will drive new consumer and business behaviour that will demand increasingly intelligent industry solutions, which, in turn, will drive trillions of dollars in opportunity for IT industry and even more for the companies that take advantage of the IOT.
