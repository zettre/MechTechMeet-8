  • About MTM

    While there are Techfests aplenty, Mech Tech Meet has a unique vibrancy. Now running in its 8th session, the meet is known for exciting mix of events. The focus is on tech expertise and preaparedness of the participating teams. Definitely not for the weak at heart or casual walk-ins. Competition is fierce, tasks are tough, tracks are rugged. The techie in you will love it. This year we are proudly adding Technovator, the international product concept contest, to the programme. We have associated with IIT Kanpur and the winners can enter directly to the finale of their events. TCS is one of our corporate partner for this season.

  • About UECU

    Ujjain Engineering College, Ujjain is one of the premier institutes of Central India known for providing valuable education in the field of engineering and technology. Established in the year 1966, the institute celebrated its Golden Jubilee last year. It is an autonomous institute that boasts of having its alumni all over the world.
