Initiatives






  • Blood Donation Camp

    "Blood is meant to circulate, Pass it around. Donate it to save someone’s life.” Accidents and medical emergencies don’t take a holiday; we need blood every minute, every day of the year. Our country, on the whole, suffers from a lack of safe blood that can be used for transfusion purposes during medical emergencies. Mech Tech Meet, noticing this urge for a safe blood for medical purpose, is organising a blood donation camp to take little step towards fulfilling this need of the society.

  • Nirmaan

    ” Education is simply the soul of the society as it passes from one generation to the other” The Nirmaan initiative is the humble beginning of Mech Tech Meet to orient the school students towards technological advancements and moreover, moulding them into a technologically responsible citizen. The initiative will draw the students out of their textbooks and introduce them to the world of technology, in case they seek to tinker with gadgets and machines.

  • Sankalp

    Sankalp is the society awareness campaign of Mech Tech Meet, initiated in 2014, to eradicate social evils. The campaign is organised every year at public places to interact with the people and turn their attention towards societal problems in an interesting way. The campaign is based on a highlighting a social issue and the ones covered till now are Women empowerment, No Addiction and Brain drain.

  • Plantation Drive

    Climate change and global warming are the demons emerged as a consequence of rapid and unplanned development of society. Apparently, deforestation is assumed to be the root cause of global warming and planting new trees is looked upon as the ultimate solution to defeat these evils. At Mech Tech Meet we believe in development while making balance with the nature. Plantation drive is one such small step towards turning the society to a greener world. Every year saplings are planted to nurture and grow them into pollution fighting heroes that promise to protect the mankind from extinction.
